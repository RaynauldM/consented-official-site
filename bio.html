The Band

Formed by vocalist Mike de Wijn in 2015, Consented made it their mission to create what they would label 'Vuig Rock'. A genre probably best compared to something like post-grunge.
In the coming years, they would play in bars and festivals to a great reception. Known for their energetic performance and hard-hitting lyrics, the band is taking over the world by storm and is only getting better.
